## Frontend Product Summary

Dwelve is a digital academic testing and performance-management platform for
schools and private learning centers. The frontend covers:

- public landing pages
- authentication
- authenticated dashboard pages
- school creation and membership context
- class/student management surfaces
- assignments and exams
- notifications
- localization

## Non-Negotiable Product Rule

Signup creates a normal user account only. School roles are created later as
memberships inside a school or learning center.
